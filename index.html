What's this?

All the goodness of the bloatless HTML5 Boilerplate: modernizr, normalize.css jQuery... – but wrapped in scss-classes.

 

Plus some niceties such as the bootstrap 4 grid, some useful mixins (like clearfix and gradients) and variables.
